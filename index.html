Movie—Rip



Movie—Rip is a forked project seeking to complete a product called AcidRip V0.14. AcidRip was abandoned by it's developer. It works very well but has indicators that don't work and, at least for me, took a long time to master due to the lack of documentation. The product has other problems, for example it can only rip films from an original DVD; I would like to see it capable of ripping from an ISO image that has already been ripped.
